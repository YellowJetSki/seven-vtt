# Subject: Resolved Clarifications - Final Canon
System Log Initialized: 2026-07-07 10:58:57

UPDATED RESOLUTIONS — Director Confirmation (July 2026)

The following pending clarifications are now resolved and incorporated into canon:

1. CHAUZY MAYOR NAME
Resolution: Byron Tudul is the canonical name. He is Strider's uncle, the Mayor of Chauzy who took him in after the Ruth incident. "Edwin Tudul" from the Old NPC list is a legacy error and should be corrected to Byron Tudul in all future references.

2. TOERN'S BROTHERS
Resolution: There are three brothers.
- Tulip Treetap — Still alive. No further details on location/condition yet.
- Tomper Treetap — Status unknown (presumed missing or dead, referenced in Toern's search).
- Tulin Treetap — Elder brother. His "goo was gone" (likely a consequence of the Quaspian mutation experiments or artificer mishap). Toern's primary quest is tied to discovering what happened to Tulin.

3. CONSOLATE vs. CONSULATE CRUSADE
Resolution: The organization is called the Consulate Crusade. It is a corrupt organization seeking control over Arkla. Emperor Hearn's bio referencing "The Consolate" is a legacy naming issue — the correct canonical name is The Consulate Crusade. Envoy Shass is a high-ranking operative. The Consulate Crusade:
- Seeks to overthrow the current Crown
- Wants to enthrone Haven Tudul as a puppet ruler
- Uses propaganda, assassinations, and alliances with pirates
- Chief Holl leads the military arm
- Balak'aptari the giant is being manipulated by Holl as a weapon
- Opposes the Lacerta and the Kolari order
