# Subject: Basilia Master File
System Log Initialized: 2026-07-07 10:56:58

BASILIA — MASTER LOCATION FILE (Current PC Location)

OVERVIEW
Allegiance: The Crown (openly) / The Consulate Crusade (secret)
Entry: Only by sea or through 2 gates (north and south). Crown recognition required. Jewl pirate symbols or Tudul ring grant entry.
Description: Massive bustling city. Three vertical layers + one secret underground. Walls of dark stone with veins, bearing Crown flags. A single gold-flagged tower peaks above the walls. Despite Crown allegiances, Jewl diamond murals are visible throughout — the city is effectively under pirate influence.

LAYER BREAKDOWN

1. PORT BASILIA (Entry Layer)
Vibe: Beautiful golden gates, red wood boardwalk, shimmering limestone streets, salt air. Covered northern port section is rougher.

Key Locations:
- The Crown Jewel (ship) — Hansel's vessel. Strip-search anyone boarding.
- Scant's Houseboat — Far end of port. Shady elven merchant. Secretly injected by Doctor Tonn; may transform into wolf-man.
- Shell-house — Lord Pooty's Crown ship. Bright red with crab symbols. Pooty Junior (nerdy crab-man, ice magic) is waiting for his father's return.
- "It's a Bargain" — Hanz's old ship, now run by his wife Pala. Neutral, grieving.
- "Shore-sd Locally" — Tizzle's potion shop. Halfling thief. Runs a street urchin ring. His potions are stolen from Doctor Tonn. One munchkin mutated and died — Tizzle killed it himself (foreshadowing his hidden strength).
- "Tackle and More" — Cleavus (chimpanzee man). Masquerades as fishing shop. Password: "This tackle is a bore.. Can you show me more." Leads to wine cellar connected to Basilia Cellar for black market magic items.

2. LOFT BASILIA (Government/Upper Layer)
Accessed via Crown Bubble service (elevator). Quiet, elegant, gold-flecked cobblestone.

Key Locations:
- Basilia Manor — Counselor Quessel's mansion. Massive, coliseum-like with glass windows and marble columns. Contains Quessel's office/court, Crown holding cells (Lord Pooty's prison), and banquet hall.
- VIP Quarters — Fancy tavern/restaurant. Chef Pascal. Only accessible with Captain Pavel's endorsement. Mouse flutist Palch. Monk Hane (Kolari, knows of Duku) can be found here.
- Basilia Printing Press — Owned by Oakleff Ogdemire (spectacled blue Orc). Loyal to Crown; outraged his press was used for propaganda.
- Shops: Basilian Antiques (split quarterstaff, 6-giant vase, reptilian-eye coin), Sky High Sales (health potion, wind-carved longbow, strange goo), Carter the Charter (map shop, needs charcoal from Doctor Tonn's lab).

3. LOWER BASILIA (General Population Layer)
Slopes down from the gates toward the port. Dense market district. Smells of spices and fish.

Key Locations:
- The Rusty Hook Inn — Armand's tavern. Front: Crown guards drinking. Back: gambling den where guards and pirates mingle. Armand (Envoy Shass secretly) recruits for Consulate Crusade here.
- Grifter Alley — Thieves' turf. Kort's goons run it. Reece (ginger urchin pickpocket) leads players here. Connects to Port Basilia near Tizzle's shop.
- Market District:
  - Spice Shack — Magdooga (swordfish-person). His Quaspian spices were stolen by Doctor Tonn.
  - Punch and Kick — Kolari-adjacent martial arts school. Teacher: Peck (crow-person). Comments on Wendy's Kolari attire.
  - Market Game Stalls: Archery range (Heest, Crown Guard), Find the Ball (Gabby), Arm wrestle Gambango (goldfish-man), Plinky's card game (mouse), Card tower game (Tinko the gnome).
- Crown Bubble Stations — East: Eustace Hampton. West: Austace Hampton (twin brother). White cape, drooping white moustache. 10 Assarions per trip. Reason for visiting Loft required.

4. THE BASILIA CELLAR (Secret Underground Layer)
Labyrinthine tunnels beneath the city. Serves as Jewl Pirates' smuggling vault and prison.
- Connected to Tackle and More via Cleavus's wine cellar.
- Lord Pooty is held here.
- Doctor Tonn conducts experiments in a lab down here (location of charcoal for Carter the Charter).
- Contains Kort's operational base.

KEY NPCS IN BASILIA

Counselor Quessel — Corrupt Crown counselor in Jewl's pocket. Smug. Has letters from H.T. (Haven Tudul) on his desk. Open listening device. Resents taking orders from Haven.

Captain Pavel — Crown Guard captain. Depressed about corruption. Secretly allied with the Old Crown faction. Helps the party escape after the prison break. Gifts them a cloaking item.

Dreaded Pirate Jewl — Red-haired pirate captain. Wields water magic via the Cleopas emerald (contains her love, Herald). Scar on left face from kraken. Squid familiar on shoulder. Works for Arteme under duress.

Hansel — Jewl's second-in-command. Gaston-like. Resistant to magic. Boxer fighter. Dumb, confident, strong.

Kort — Orc enforcer. Runs goons in Lower Basilia and the Cellar. Hook-hand. Leads the prison encounter where he threatens Lord Pooty.

Tizzle — Halfling potion seller. Thief. Runs street urchins. Knows about Doctor Tonn's dangerous potions. Hidden immense strength.

Cleavus — Chimpanzee pirate. Runs black market magic items through fake fishing shop.

Armand (Envoy Shass) — Bartender at Rusty Hook. Secretly a Quaspian assassin for the Consulate Crusade. Bandages on arms, turban. Recruits the party to assassinate Quessel at sea.

Oakleff Ogdemire — Blue spectacled Orc. Runs the Printing Press. Loyal to Crown.

Pooty Junior — Crab-man, Lord Pooty's son. Neurotic, bookish. Rapier + ice magic. Docked at Shell-house.

CURRENT SESSION STATE (Post-Session 4 Setup)

The party has completed (or is about to complete) the trial, the Jewl brawl, the manor prison break, and Pooty's rescue. Pavel has given them a cloaking item. Their choice: flee Basilia permanently, or sneak back to the Rusty Hook.

If they return to the Rusty Hook:
- Armand reveals himself as Envoy Shass.
- He offers them the mission: stow away on Quessel's ship to Auralis and assassinate him at sea.
- Reward: Doctor Tonn's dangerous mutation potions (dramatic irony — these are the same potions causing the city's anomalies).

SESSION 4 PIVOT POINTS
- PART 1: Tizzle's warning (mutated munchkin) — if players visit before court
- PART 2: Trial skill challenge — determines Quessel's mood
- PART 3: Jewl's non-lethal brawl — THEFT MECHANIC: Jewl steals an item from any PC dropped to 0 HP
- PART 4: Manor Cellars dungeon crawl — stealth + puzzles
- PART 5: Kort boss fight — HOSTAGE MECHANIC: kill a goon, Kort attacks Pooty
- PART 6: Pavel's escape + cloaking item
- PART 7: Shass's reveal + assassination mission offer

UPCOMING: If the party accepts, Session 5 begins with a stealth-heist to board Quessel's ship bound for Auralis.
