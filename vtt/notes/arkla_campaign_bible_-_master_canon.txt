## 1. WORLD OVERVIEW

**Setting:** The world of Arkla. A fantasy sandbox with political intrigue, pirate factions, corrupt institutions, and ancient Nevari mysteries.

### Major Locations (Canon)
- **Basilia** — A port city, current hub of action. Divided into districts including the Cellar District (slums/underbelly). Home to the Rusty Hook Inn.
- **Auralis** — A royal seat of power. Origin point of Edmund "Strider" Tudul.
- **Chauzy** — A smaller town. Mayor: **Byron Tudul** (Strider's uncle).
- **The Jewl Coast** — Pirate territory. Home to the Jewl Pirates.

## 3. CORE FACTIONS

### The Consulate Crusade
- A corrupt organization operating in Basilia.
- Envoy **Shass** is their operative embedded at the Rusty Hook Inn (disguised as barkeep/ally).
- Their goals and full hierarchy — [AWAITING DIRECTOR DEFINITION].

### The Tudul Family
- A noble/political family with stretched influence across Auralis, Chauzy, and potentially Basilia.
- **Byron Tudul** — Mayor of Chauzy. Strider's uncle. Canon confirmed.
- **Haven Tudor** — Strider's brother. Implicated in the death of their sister Ruth Tudor. Strider fled Auralis because of him.
- **Ruth Tudor** — Deceased sister of Strider. Her death is a key trauma in Strider's backstory.
- **Edmund "Strider" Tudul** — PC. A traumatized prince on the run, seeking to make his own name.

### The Jewl Pirates
- Led by the **Dreaded Pirate Jewl**.
- Seeking a dormant Nevari (location key contained in a cane stolen from Mayor Byron Tudul).
- Jewl wants to trade the Nevari to someone named **Arteme**.
- Overlapping but distinct goals from the Consulate Crusade in Basilia.

### The Crown Guard
- The official law enforcement of Basilia.
- **Captain Pavel** — A regretful officer. Cannot interfere directly in court politics. Has sympathy for the party. Canon gift-giver of the *Driftcloak of the Saltwind*.

### Doctor Tonn & His Experiments
- A shadowy figure conducting biological/magical experimentation.
- His potions are dangerous to organic life (confirmed by Tizzle, a mutated stable munchkin).
- The *Vial of Tonn's Essence* is in Shass's possession, offered as a "reward" — grants short-term power with risk of exhaustion and mutation.
